Welcome to SDA's Robotics Club: Team Paradox!

We at FIRST Team Paradox 2102 have dedicated ourselves to becoming and guiding the next generation of problem solvers. For the past decade, engineers, artists, machinists, managers, programmers, writers and makers have worked together to create an environment where creativity and passions bloom.


We are a FIRST (For the Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) FRC (FIRST Robotics Competition) team based at San Dieguito High School Academy (SDA). Our team is based off the values of STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art and Math) education and uphold FIRST core values of Gracious Professionalism and Coopertition in everything we do.


On this website you can discover more about FIRST and how our team works to go above and beyond FIRST’s missions.


If you have any questions, comments, or concerns, feel free to contact us through the links at our Contacts Page.
