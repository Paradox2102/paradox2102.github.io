Welcome to SDA Robotics: Team Paradox!

We are engineers, artists, machinists, managers, programmers, writers, makers, and problem solvers. Our family has grown from a team of 2 students in 2006 to 55 students, 20 mentors and countless parents and supporters. However our purpose has been ever lasting throughout the generations.


We’re a bunch of teenagers, who somehow have time to spare and we’re going to solve a problem.

Supporting STEAM (science technology, engineering, art and math) education we strive to provide an environment for people of all ages to discover their passion. Starting out from the platform of FIRST (for the inspiration and recognition of science and technology) FRC (FIRST robotics competition) our team has become so much more than just finishing a robot by the end of build season. Using FIRST as a base, we strive to expand on the values of FIRST and find something within the team to make our own personal mission. Whether that’s finishing the competition robot in time to program an autonomous, starting a new FLL team or hosting our own STEAM fair, we push ourselves to be imaginative, innovative and adventurous. We at FIRST Team Paradox 2102 have dedicated ourselves to making and becoming the next generation of problem solvers.


And well this is our website. Here you can find out everything about our team with a little reading and a lot of pretty pictures.
